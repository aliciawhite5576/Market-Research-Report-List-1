<p><p>The fire-rated building material market consists of various types of products. Sealants & fillers are used to seal gaps and joints, preventing the spread of fire. Mortar is fire-resistant and used in construction to hold bricks or blocks together. Sheets/boards with fire-resistant properties provide thermal insulation and structural integrity. Spray materials are applied to surfaces to create a fire-resistant barrier. Preformed devices are pre-manufactured components used in fireproofing systems. Putty is a fire-resistant compound used to seal openings around penetrations. Cast-in devices are integrated into the building structure to prevent fire spread. Other fire-rated materials include coatings, fabrics, and cable wraps.</p></p>
