<p><p>Flame retardant masterbatches are additives used in various industries to enhance the fire resistance of materials. They come in different types based on the polymers they are compatible with, such as PS (polystyrene), PP (polypropylene), PA (polyamide), and PC (polycarbonate). PS flame retardant masterbatches are suitable for applications involving polystyrene-based products, while PP masterbatches are used with polypropylene materials. Similarly, PA and PC masterbatches are designed for enhancing flame resistance in polyamide and polycarbonate-based products, respectively. These additives contribute to reducing the flammability and increasing safety in the specified polymer materials.</p></p>
