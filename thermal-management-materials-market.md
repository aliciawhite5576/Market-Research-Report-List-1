<p><p>The thermal management materials market can be classified into three types: metal thermal management materials, electronic thermal management materials, and others. Metal thermal management materials are used for heat dissipation in electronic devices such as heat sinks and heat spreaders. Electronic thermal management materials are utilized for the efficient thermal conduction within electronic components like ICs and PCBs. The "others" category includes materials like phase change materials, thermal interface materials, and thermal greases that are used in various thermal management applications to enhance heat dissipation and thermal conduction.</p></p>
