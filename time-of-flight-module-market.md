<p><p>A Time-of-Flight (ToF) module is a device or sensor that measures the time it takes for light to travel to an object and back to the sensor. It uses infrared light to calculate the distance between the sensor and the object by measuring the time it takes for the light to reflect off the object and return to the sensor. ToF modules have various applications, including 3D sensing, gesture recognition, augmented reality, depth mapping, and object tracking.</p><p>The future outlook for the Time-of-Flight Module Market is promising. The increasing demand for advanced and precise ranging and distance measurement technology is driving the growth of this market. ToF modules offer high accuracy in measuring distances, making them valuable in applications such as autonomous vehicles, industrial robotics, and smartphones. Additionally, the growing adoption of ToF modules in consumer electronics, gaming devices, and medical equipment further contributes to market growth.</p><p>The current outlook of the Time-of-Flight Module Market is also positive. The market is witnessing steady growth due to rising investments in research and development by key market players to improve the performance of ToF modules.
