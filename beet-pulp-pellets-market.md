These pellets are often preferred by livestock owners due to their sweeter flavor. On the other hand, ordinary beet pulp granules are made without the addition of molasses and are a more common choice for animal feed. Both types of pellets are widely used in the livestock industry for their nutritional value and ease of storage.</p></p>
