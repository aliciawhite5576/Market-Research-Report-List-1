<p><strong>The Microduct Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thick Walled Ducts (TWD)</li><li>Tight Protected Ducts (TPD)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The microduct cable market includes two types of duct systems: Thick Walled Ducts (TWD) and Tight Protected Ducts (TPD). TWDs are designed with a thick outer wall that provides enhanced durability and protection against external elements, making them suitable for harsh environments. On the other hand, TPDs are constructed with a tight protective layer that offers excellent flexibility and space efficiency, allowing for easier installation and increased cable capacity within limited infrastructure. Both types cater to different needs in the microduct cable market, providing options for various applications and requirements.</p></p>
