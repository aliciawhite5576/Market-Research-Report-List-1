<p><p>Stearic acid is a saturated fatty acid that is derived from animal and vegetable fats and oils. It is a white, waxy solid with a mild odor. In the plastics industry, stearic acid is used as a lubricant in the production of PVC and other plastic materials. It helps in improving the processability of these materials.
