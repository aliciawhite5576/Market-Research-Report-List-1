<p><p>Jockey Boxes are portable beer dispensing systems commonly used in outdoor events and parties. They come in two main market types: coil cooler and cold-plate cooler. Coil cooler jockey boxes work by circulating beer through a long coil immersed in ice, cooling it down before dispensing. Cold-plate cooler jockey boxes, on the other hand, have a cold plate attached to the bottom of the box, where the beer is chilled. Both types offer a convenient and efficient way to keep beer cold and ready to serve while on the go.</p></p>
