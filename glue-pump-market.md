<p><p>The glue pump market is categorized into two types: manual and electric. Manual glue pumps require the operator to manually pump the glue out of the pump, usually by using a hand lever or crank. These pumps are often utilized in smaller-scale applications or situations where portability is important. On the other hand, electric glue pumps are powered by electricity and require minimal human effort. They are more commonly used in larger-scale operations or where a consistent and continuous flow of glue is required.</p></p>
