<p><p>The wireless wearable speaker market can be broadly classified into two types based on their battery configurations: built-in battery and external battery. Built-in battery speakers come with an integrated rechargeable battery, eliminating the need for external power sources. They are convenient and portable, allowing users to wear them comfortably and move freely. On the other hand, external battery speakers rely on replaceable or rechargeable batteries that are separate from the device. This type provides flexibility as users can carry extra batteries for extended usage but may require an additional power source.</p></p>
