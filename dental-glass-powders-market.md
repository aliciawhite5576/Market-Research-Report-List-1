<p><p>Dental glass powders are categorized into two main types: inert glass powders and reactive glass powders. Inert glass powders do not undergo any chemical reactions when used in dental applications, making them stable and resistant to changes. They are commonly used as fillers or reinforcements in dental materials. On the other hand, reactive glass powders undergo chemical reactions, which allows them to bond with other dental components and enhance the overall mechanical properties of dental materials. These powders are commonly used in restorative and adhesive dental applications.</p></p>
