<p><p>Tissue ablation devices are used to destroy or remove abnormal or diseased tissues in the body. There are several types of tissue ablation devices available in the market. Radiofrequency ablation uses heat generated by radio waves to destroy tissues. Ultrasound ablation uses high-frequency sound waves to destroy tissues. Laser-based ablation utilizes laser energy to vaporize or destroy tissues. Cryoablation devices use extremely cold temperatures to freeze and destroy tissues. Other types of tissue ablation devices include microwave ablation and irreversible electroporation. These devices offer various options for physicians to safely and effectively ablate tissues.</p></p>
