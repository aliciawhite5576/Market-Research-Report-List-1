<p><p>Silicon Carbide (SiC) is a synthetic material made from a combination of silicon and carbon. These wafers offer several advantages over traditional silicon wafers, including higher breakdown voltage, lower power losses, and higher temperature operation.</p><p>The future outlook of the SiC wafer market is highly promising.
