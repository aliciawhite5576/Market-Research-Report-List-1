<p><ul><li>Porous Hydroxyapatite Ceramics</li><li>Dense Hydroxyapatite Ceramics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hydroxyapatite ceramics are a type of ceramic material that is composed of hydroxyapatite, a mineral found in bone tissue. There are two main types of hydroxyapatite ceramics in the market - porous and dense. Porous hydroxyapatite ceramics have open pores within their structure, allowing for the infiltration of body fluids and cells, making them suitable for applications such as bone grafting and tissue engineering. On the other hand, dense hydroxyapatite ceramics have a solid structure with no open pores, making them more suitable for load-bearing applications such as dental implants and prosthetics.</p></p>
