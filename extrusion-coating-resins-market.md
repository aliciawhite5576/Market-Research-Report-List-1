<p><p>Extrusion coating resins are used in various applications such as packaging, labeling, and laminating processes. LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) is a commonly used type of resin due to its excellent flexibility and barrier properties. EVA (Ethylene Vinyl Acetate) resin is known for its exceptional adhesion and elasticity, making it suitable for applications requiring good heat resistance. PP (Polypropylene) resin is preferred for its stiffness and high-temperature resistance. Other types of resins used in the extrusion coating market include polyethylene terephthalate (PET), polyvinyl chloride (PVC), and ethylene copolymers. These resins cater to various industry requirements and provide specific properties in the coated products.</p></p>
