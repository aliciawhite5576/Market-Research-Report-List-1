<p><p>Cutting fluid refers to a type of fluid used in industrial processes to cool or lubricate tools during metalworking operations. Cooling fluids, as the name suggests, are primarily used to dissipate heat generated during cutting, preventing overheating and damage to tools. Lubrication fluids, on the other hand, are employed to reduce friction between the tool and workpiece, minimizing wear and tear on both. These fluids play crucial roles in enhancing cutting efficiency, extending tool life, and improving overall productivity in various metal fabrication industries.</p></p>
