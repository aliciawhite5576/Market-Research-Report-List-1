<p><p>Sedan and hatchback wheel speed sensor market types include Hall type and magnetic electric type. Hall type sensors utilize a Hall effect sensor to measure the rotational speed of the wheels. They have a higher level of accuracy and are commonly used in high-performance vehicles. On the other hand, magnetic electric type sensors use magnets and electric coils to detect the wheel's speed. They are simpler in design and are usually found in budget-friendly vehicles. Both types serve the same purpose of providing information to the vehicle's anti-lock braking system and traction control system.</p></p>
