<p><p>Electric pruners are available in two types: cordless power and chargeable power. Cordless power electric pruners operate without the need for an electrical cord, providing greater freedom of movement and convenience. They are powered by batteries and can be easily transported and used in various locations. On the other hand, chargeable power electric pruners operate by plugging them into an electrical outlet to charge their internal battery. These pruners offer the advantage of uninterrupted power supply, eliminating the need for battery replacements. Both types cater to different preferences and requirements of users in the market.</p></p>
