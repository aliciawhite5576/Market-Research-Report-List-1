<p><p>The marine bilge pump market consists of two main types: electric bilge pumps and manual bilge pumps. Electric bilge pumps are powered by electricity and automatically remove water from the bilge of a boat. They are popular due to their convenience and efficiency. On the other hand, manual bilge pumps require manual operation and are typically used as backup or emergency pumps. They are simpler in design and rely on human power to remove water from the bilge. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the marine industry.</p></p>
