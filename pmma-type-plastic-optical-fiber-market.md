<p><strong>The PMMA Type Plastic Optical Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Step Index Profile</li><li>Graded Index Profile</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PMMA type plastic optical fiber market comprises two types: step index profile and graded index profile. Step index profile fibers have a uniform refractive index throughout the core, resulting in the transmission of light in a straight line. Graded index profile fibers have a changing refractive index, allowing light to travel in a curved or zigzag path, minimizing distortion and improving signal quality. Both types are widely used in various applications including telecommunications, automotive, medical, and lighting due to their flexibility, durability, and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
