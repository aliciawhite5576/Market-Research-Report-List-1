<p><p>Micronucleus kits are diagnostic tools used in cytogenetic studies to detect and quantify the formation of micronuclei, which are small nuclei formed during cell division. The kits typically contain reagents and materials necessary to prepare samples and conduct the micronucleus assay.</p><p>The micronucleus kits market is forecasted to experience significant growth in the coming years. <p><p>The Micronucleus Kits market consists of two types: In Vitro Micronucleus Assay and In Vivo Micronucleus Assay. The In Vitro Micronucleus Assay involves studying the occurrence of micronuclei in cultured cells, outside the organism. It is primarily used for genotoxicity testing and drug development. On the other hand, the In Vivo Micronucleus Assay involves analyzing micronucleus formation in living organisms, such as animals, under controlled conditions. This assay is commonly used in toxicology studies, environmental monitoring, and assessing the genotoxic effects of various substances.</p></p>
