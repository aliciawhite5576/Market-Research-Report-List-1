<p><p>The automotive cooling fan coupling market can be categorized into two types: the plate sensor system type and the coil sensor system type. The plate sensor system type utilizes a plate sensor that is integrated into the coupling structure to detect and measure temperature changes, thus controlling the fan speed accordingly. On the other hand, the coil sensor system type employs a coil sensor that monitors temperature and adjusts the fan speed based on the received signals. Both types play vital roles in maintaining the optimal temperature of automotive engines for efficient operation.</p></p>
