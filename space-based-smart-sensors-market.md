Radiation-hardened sensors are designed to withstand high levels of radiation and are commonly used in space environments where radiation levels are intense, such as near nuclear reactors or in deep space missions. On the other hand, radiation-tolerant sensors are able to operate in space environments with lower radiation levels, making them suitable for applications such as satellite communication or Earth observation. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in ensuring reliable and accurate performance in space-based systems.</p></p>
