<p><p>Functional pet foods are specially formulated to provide additional health benefits to pets beyond basic nutrition. As pet owners become more conscious about preventive healthcare for their pets, they are willing to invest in high-quality, specialized pet foods that can address specific health concerns.</p><p>Additionally, the growing pet population and the rising trend of pet humanization are further fueling the market growth. Pet owners are treating their pets as family members, leading to increased spending on premium pet food products. This trend is expected to continue in the future, driving the demand for functional pet foods.</p><p>Furthermore, advancements in pet food technology and the introduction of innovative ingredients are likely to create new opportunities in the market.
