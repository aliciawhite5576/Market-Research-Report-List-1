<p><p>Intravenous (IV) iron drugs market includes different types of medications used to treat iron deficiency anemia. Ferric Carboxymaltose, Iron Sucrose, and Iron Dextran are three main types of IV iron drugs available in the market. Ferric Carboxymaltose is a complex carbohydrate that delivers iron to the body, while Iron Sucrose is an iron salt complexed with sucrose. Iron Dextran, on the other hand, is an iron complexed with a sugar polymer. These drugs are administered intravenously to replenish iron levels in the body and improve symptoms of anemia.</p></p>
