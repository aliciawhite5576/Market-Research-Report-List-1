Aliphatic hydrocarbon solvents are extensively used in the formulation of paints and coatings due to their excellent solvency and evaporation properties. The growing construction and automotive sectors, particularly in emerging economies, are driving the demand for paints and coatings, thus fueling the market growth.</p><p>Furthermore, the increasing environmental concerns and stringent regulations regarding the use of volatile organic compounds (VOCs) have led to a shift from aromatic hydrocarbon solvents to their aliphatic counterparts. Aliphatic hydrocarbon solvents have lower toxicity and lower VOC content, making them a preferred choice for various applications, thus driving the market growth.</p><p>In terms of regional analysis, North America and Europe are mature markets for aliphatic hydrocarbon solvents, with a significant presence of major manufacturers.
