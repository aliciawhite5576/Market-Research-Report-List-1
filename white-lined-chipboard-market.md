<p><p>White lined chipboard is a type of paper-based board that is commonly used in the packaging industry. It is available in two market types: single layer coating and double layer coating. </p><p>In single layer coating, the chipboard is coated with a thin layer of white paper on one side only, providing a smooth and glossy finish. This type is typically used for applications where only one side of the board will be visible.</p><p>In double layer coating, the chipboard is coated with a layer of white paper on both sides, offering a uniform appearance and enhanced durability. This type is commonly used for applications that require a high-quality finish on both sides of the board, such as premium packaging or graphic displays.</p></p>
