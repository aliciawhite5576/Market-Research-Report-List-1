<p><p>Wood primers are used to prepare wooden surfaces for painting or staining. There are three main types of wood primers in the market. Oil-based primers are popular for their durability and moisture resistance, making them suitable for exterior wood surfaces. Water-based primers are eco-friendly and have low odor, making them ideal for interior applications. Shellac-based primers provide excellent stain blocking properties and are often used on wood with heavy stains or knots. Each primer type offers unique benefits and should be chosen based on the specific wood surface and project requirements.</p></p>
