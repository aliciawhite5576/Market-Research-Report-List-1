<p><p>The fire protection materials market encompasses various types of materials used to prevent or retard the spread of fire. Fire-resistant boards are boards made from non-combustible materials used for insulation. Sealants are fire-resistant substances applied to gaps and joints to prevent fire penetration. Vermiculite cement sprays are fireproof plaster-like mixtures used for insulation and fire protection. Fiber sprays provide fire resistance to structural elements. Dry lining systems are fire-resistant panels used for walls and ceilings. Mineral wool is a type of insulation material with excellent fire resistance. Intumescent paints and coatings expand when exposed to heat, forming a protective char layer to inhibit fire spread.</p></p>
