<p><p>The plastic pellet market can be categorized into various types based on the materials used. Each type of plastic pellet has unique characteristics and applications. LDPE is flexible and used in packaging, while PET is widely used for beverage bottles. HDPE is known for its strength and is used in pipes and containers. PE serves various industries, while PVC is popular in construction. PP finds uses in automotive and packaging, while ABS is known for its durability and used in electronics. Other materials include specialized types like polystyrene and polycarbonate.</p></p>
