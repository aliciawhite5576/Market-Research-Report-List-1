<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The automotive seating market consists of three main types: split seat, bench seat, and split bench seat. A split seat refers to a seat configuration where the front passenger and driver seats are separate entities. A bench seat, on the other hand, is a single continuous seat that spans the entire width of the vehicle and accommodates multiple passengers. A split bench seat combines both concepts, featuring a divided seat with individual segments for the driver and front passenger, while still maintaining a continuous structure for the rear passengers.</p></p>
