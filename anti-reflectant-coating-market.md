<p><ul><li>Single-layer Interference Coating</li><li>Multi-layer Interference Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anti-reflectant coating market includes two main types: single-layer interference coating and multi-layer interference coating. A single-layer interference coating consists of a single thin layer applied onto a substrate to reduce reflection by destructive interference of light waves. On the other hand, a multi-layer interference coating involves multiple layers of thin films with varying refractive indices to enhance light transmission by minimizing reflection. Both types of coatings aim to improve optical performance and reduce glare in various applications such as eyeglasses, camera lenses, and solar panels.</p></p>
