These chains are lightweight, easy to install, and offer good grip on snow-covered roads while minimizing potential damage to the tires and the road surface.</p><p>Both metal and nonmetal snow chains serve the same purpose of enhancing traction on snowy terrains, but they differ in terms of construction materials and specific features.</p></p>
