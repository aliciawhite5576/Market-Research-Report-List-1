<p><p>Online catering order platforms can be categorized into two main market types: restaurant-controlled and independent market platforms. </p><p>Restaurant-controlled platforms are operated and managed by a specific restaurant or a chain of restaurants. These platforms provide an online ordering system exclusively for the restaurant(s) affiliated with them. Customers can directly order food, customize their orders, and make payments through these platforms.</p><p>On the other hand, independent market platforms serve as a marketplace connecting various restaurants and caterers with customers. These platforms offer a wide range of options from different establishments, allowing customers to browse and order food from multiple vendors in one place. Payments and delivery logistics are usually handled by the independent market platform itself.</p></p>
