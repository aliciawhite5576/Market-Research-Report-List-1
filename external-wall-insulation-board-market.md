<p><p>The External Wall Insulation Board market consists of three types: Polystyrene Board, Rock Wool Board, and PU Board. Polystyrene Board is made of expanded polystyrene and provides excellent thermal insulation. Rock Wool Board is made from molten rock and offers superior fire resistance and sound insulation properties. PU Board, derived from polyurethane, has high insulation performance and is lightweight. These boards are used for external wall insulation projects and provide energy efficiency, weather protection, and noise reduction benefits to buildings.</p></p>
