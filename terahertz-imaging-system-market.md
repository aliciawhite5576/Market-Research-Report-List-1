<p><p>Terahertz imaging systems can be categorized into two main types: passive terahertz imaging and active terahertz imaging. </p><p>Passive terahertz imaging involves detecting and capturing the naturally occurring terahertz waves emitted by objects. It relies on the ability of terahertz waves to penetrate certain materials, revealing hidden objects or details that may be missed by other imaging techniques.</p><p>In contrast, active terahertz imaging systems emit their own terahertz waves and measure the reflected or transmitted signals. This allows for more control over the imaging process and the ability to generate higher resolution images, making it particularly useful in security, military, and industrial applications.</p></p>
