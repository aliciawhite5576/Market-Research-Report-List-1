<p><p>The corrugated carton ink market comprises three main types: water-based inks, solvent-based inks, and UV-cured inks. Water-based inks are environmentally friendly and widely used, offering good print quality and fast drying time. Solvent-based inks are oil-based and provide excellent durability and adhesion, but they can be toxic and have high VOC levels. UV-cured inks use ultraviolet light to instantly dry, resulting in high-quality prints with no volatile emissions. Each ink type caters to specific needs and preferences, considering factors such as environmental impact, print quality, and drying time.</p></p>
