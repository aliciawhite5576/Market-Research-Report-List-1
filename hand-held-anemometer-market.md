The professional segment is projected to hold a larger market share as hand-held anemometers are extensively used by meteorologists, researchers, and field technicians.</p><p>Geographically, North America is anticipated to have the largest market share in the hand-held anemometer market, followed by Europe and Asia-Pacific.
