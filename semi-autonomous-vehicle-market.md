<p><p>The semi-autonomous vehicle market comprises two levels: Level 2 Autonomous Vehicles and Level 3 Autonomous Vehicles. Level 2 vehicles are equipped with advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS) that can control steering, acceleration, and braking, but require constant human supervision. Level 3 vehicles take a step further by offering conditional automation, where the car can handle certain aspects of driving without human intervention under defined conditions. However, drivers must still be prepared to take over control whenever necessary. Both market types exhibit increasing levels of automation, enhancing safety and driving comfort while keeping driver involvement as a crucial component.</p></p>
