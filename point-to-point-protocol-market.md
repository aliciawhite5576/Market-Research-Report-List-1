<p><p>Point-to-Point Protocol (PPP) is a widely used networking protocol that establishes a direct connection between two devices over a physical link. In the market, there are different types of PPP, including Multilink PPP and Multiclass PPP. Multilink PPP allows multiple physical links to be combined into a single logical link, providing increased bandwidth and reliability. On the other hand, Multiclass PPP enables the transmission of multiple classes of traffic over different logical links, allowing for prioritization of different types of data based on their importance or characteristics.</p></p>
