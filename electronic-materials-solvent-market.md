These include PGMEA, PGME, EGBE, DEGBE, 2-heptanone, 2-pentanone, n-Butyl Acetate, and others. PGMEA and PGME are glycol ether solvents commonly used as photoresist solvents. EGBE and DEGBE are ethylene glycol ether solvents used in cleaning solutions and as co-solvents. 2-heptanone and 2-pentanone are ketone solvents used as rinsing and cleaning agents. n-Butyl Acetate is an acetate solvent employed in the formulation of electronic coatings. These solvents offer key properties such as high solvency power, low toxicity, and stability, making them valuable in the electronics industry.</p></p>
