<p><p>Disaster Recovery-as-a-Service (DRaaS) is a cloud-based solution that helps organizations recover their systems and data in the event of a disaster. The market types for DRaaS include premises-to-cloud and cloud-to-cloud. </p><p>Premises-to-cloud DRaaS involves replicating an organization's on-premises data and applications to the cloud, providing a backup and recovery solution in case of a disaster. This allows businesses to restore their systems and resume operations quickly without relying on their own physical infrastructure.</p><p>Cloud-to-cloud DRaaS, on the other hand, focuses on replicating data and applications between different cloud platforms. This allows businesses to protect their data and applications by keeping copies in multiple cloud environments, ensuring availability and minimizing the risk of data loss.</p></p>
