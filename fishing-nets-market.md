<p><strong>The Fishing Nets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Knotted Nets</li><li>Knotless Nets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fishing nets are categorized into two main types in the market: knotted nets and knotless nets. Knotted nets are made by joining intersecting strands or fibers with knots, which provide strength and durability. They are commonly used for capturing large fish species as the knots prevent the net from breaking. On the other hand, knotless nets are made using a special weaving technique that eliminates knots. These nets are preferred for catching small fish species as they reduce the chances of the catch getting entangled or harmed.</p></p>
