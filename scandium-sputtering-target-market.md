<p><p>The Scandium Sputtering Target market includes two main types: Rotary Type and Non-Rotatable Type. The Rotary Type involves targets that are mounted on a rotating plate, enabling even erosion and prolonging the target's lifespan. It is suitable for large-scale sputtering applications. In contrast, the Non-Rotatable Type refers to targets that do not rotate during the sputtering process. They are commonly used in applications requiring high precision or where rotating targets are not feasible due to size or structural constraints. Both types cater to different market demands and applications.</p></p>
