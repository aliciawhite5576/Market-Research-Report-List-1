Additionally, with the growing trend of customization and innovative designs in furniture and interior decor, MDF provides flexibility and ease of shaping and processing.</p><p>Moreover, the rising construction activities in developing economies, along with the growth of the furniture industry, are further fueling the demand for MDF.
