<p><p>The Perfluoroelastomer Polymer Market includes various types such as O-Ring, Gasket, and Other Seals. O-Rings are circular-shaped elastomers designed to create a seal between two parts, preventing leakage of fluids or gases. Gaskets are specifically shaped pieces of material placed between two surfaces to ensure a tight seal and prevent leakage. Other Seals encompass a wide range of sealing products beyond O-Rings and gaskets, including lip seals, diaphragms, and custom-designed seals. These products find applications in various industries, such as automotive, chemical processing, and oil and gas, where reliable sealing is crucial.</p></p>
