<p><strong>The Micro Automotive Relay Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plug-in Relay</li><li>PCB Relay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The micro automotive relay market includes various types such as plug-in relays and PCB relays. Plug-in relays are widely used in automotive applications as they can be easily fitted into relay sockets. These relays are designed to plug into a mating socket and connect with the wiring harness. On the other hand, PCB relays are directly mounted on printed circuit boards. They are compact, cost-effective, and provide stability in high vibration environments. Both types of relays play a crucial role in controlling the electrical components and systems in automobiles.</p></p>
