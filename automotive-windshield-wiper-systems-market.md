<p><p>Automotive windshield wiper systems are an essential component of vehicles used to clear rain, snow, or debris from the windshield, ensuring better visibility while driving. A combination of sensors and controls is also incorporated to detect precipitation and control the speed and movement of the wipers.</p><p>The market analysis of the automotive windshield wiper systems indicates a positive growth trajectory. Factors such as an increase in vehicle production, rising concerns for road safety, and stringent regulations mandating the use of wiper systems are driving the market growth.
