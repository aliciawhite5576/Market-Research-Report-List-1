<p><p>The plastic colorant market consists of three main types: inorganic pigments, organic pigments, and dyes. Inorganic pigments are derived from minerals and are known for their opacity and resistance to heat and chemicals. Organic pigments are synthetic compounds and offer a wide range of vibrant colors. Dyes, on the other hand, are mainly water-soluble and penetrate the plastic material, resulting in transparent or translucent colored products. The choice between these types depends on the desired color, transparency, heat resistance, and chemical stability required for the specific plastic application.</p></p>
