<p><p>The microscope illuminator market is divided into two types: halogen and LED. Halogen illuminators use halogen bulbs that produce a bright, warm, and steady light source. They are cost-effective but require more maintenance. On the other hand, LED illuminators use light-emitting diodes that offer energy efficiency, longer lifespan, and adjustable intensity. They are more expensive initially but save costs in the long run due to their durability. LED illuminators are becoming increasingly popular in the market due to their eco-friendly nature and technological advancements.</p></p>
