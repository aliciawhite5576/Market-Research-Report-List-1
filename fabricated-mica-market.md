<p><p>Fabricated mica, also known as synthetic or man-made mica, is a substitute for natural mica. It finds applications in various industries, including electronics, cosmetics, automotive, and paint.</p><p>In terms of market analysis, the fabricated mica market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years.
