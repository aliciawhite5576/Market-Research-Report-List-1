<p><p>In the aerospace industry, lapping machines are used for precision flat surface finishing of components. The market for lapping machines is divided into two main types: vertical lapping machines and horizontal lapping machines. Vertical lapping machines have a vertically oriented workpiece holder, allowing for efficient gravity-assisted material removal. Horizontal lapping machines, on the other hand, have a horizontally oriented workpiece holder, which is suitable for larger components. Both types of machines offer precise and controlled abrasion methods for achieving smooth and accurate surfaces in aerospace manufacturing processes.</p></p>
