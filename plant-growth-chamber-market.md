<p><p>Plant growth chambers are specialized environments designed for studying and regulating plant growth. They have limited space but provide precise temperature, humidity, and lighting control. On the other hand, Walk-In growth chambers are larger, allowing researchers to experiment with a wider range of plant species and conduct more extensive studies. These chambers have larger doors and spacious interiors, making it easier to analyze and manipulate the plants within.</p></p>
