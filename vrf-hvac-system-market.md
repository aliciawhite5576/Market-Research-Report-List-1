<p><p>The VRF HVAC system market offers different types of systems, including the Horizontal Branch Pipe and Vertical Branch Pipe systems. The Horizontal Branch Pipe system allows for the distribution of refrigerant to multiple indoor units through a common pipe installed horizontally. On the other hand, the Vertical Branch Pipe system distributes refrigerant vertically, using separate pipes for each indoor unit. Both systems provide flexibility and efficient cooling or heating, while the choice between them depends on the specific requirements of the building and the preferences of the users.</p></p>
