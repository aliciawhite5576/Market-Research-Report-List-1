<p><p>A bipolar converter transformer is a type of transformer specifically designed for high-voltage direct current (HVDC) applications. Bipolar converter transformers play a crucial role in HVDC transmission systems by converting the alternating current (AC) generated by the power station into direct current (DC) for efficient transmission over long distances. These transformers are designed to operate at high voltages and currents, ensuring the safe and reliable conversion of power.</p><p>The bipolar converter transformer market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
