<p><p>Fischer-Tropsch (FT) waxes can be categorized into two market types: high-melt FT waxes and medium-melt FT waxes. High-melt FT waxes have a higher melting point, typically above 80°C, and are used in applications where a higher resistance to heat and pressure is required, such as in the production of hot-melt adhesives or rubber compounds. On the other hand, medium-melt FT waxes have a lower melting point, usually between 40-80°C, and find applications in products like candles, packaging, or coatings, where lower heat resistance is sufficient.</p></p>
