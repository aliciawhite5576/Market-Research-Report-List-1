<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Rotary Tube Furnaces Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Zone Rotary Tube Furnaces</li><li>Dual-Zone Rotary Tube Furnaces</li><li>Multi-Zone Rotary Tube Furnaces</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rotary tube furnaces are classified into three types based on their heating zones. Single zone rotary tube furnaces have only one heating zone, producing a uniform temperature throughout the furnace. Dual-zone rotary tube furnaces consist of two heating zones, enabling different temperature profiles within the furnace. Multi-zone rotary tube furnaces have multiple independent heating zones, allowing precise control of temperature profiles at different sections of the furnace. These different types cater to various research and industrial applications, providing flexibility and enhanced heating capabilities.</p></p>
