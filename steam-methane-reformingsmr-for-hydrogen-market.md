<p><p>Steam Methane Reforming (SMR) is a commonly used method for producing hydrogen gas (H2) on an industrial scale. This syngas is then further processed to purify the hydrogen for various applications, such as fuel cells or chemical synthesis.</p><p>The current outlook for the SMR for Hydrogen Market is promising.
