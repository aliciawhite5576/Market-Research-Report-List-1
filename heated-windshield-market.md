<p><ul><li>Tungsten Wire Heated</li><li>Conductive Layer Heated</li></ul></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Heated Windshield market comprises two main types: Tungsten Wire Heated and Conductive Layer Heated. In the Tungsten Wire Heated market, a tungsten wire is integrated into the windshield, which heats up to remove frost or ice. This method is typically used in aftermarket installations. On the other hand, the Conductive Layer Heated market involves incorporating a conductive layer within the windshield, which is connected to a power source, heating the entire windshield surface uniformly. This type is commonly used in original equipment manufacturers (OEM) installations.</p></p>
