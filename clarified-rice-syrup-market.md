<p><p>Clarified Rice Syrup has two main market types: white rice and brown rice. White rice syrup is made from refined white rice, where the bran and germ have been removed. It is light in color and has a mild flavor. On the other hand, brown rice syrup is produced from whole grain brown rice, which retains the bran and germ. It has a darker color and a richer, nuttier flavor. These market types cater to different consumer preferences and are used as sweeteners or food ingredients in various food and beverage products.</p></p>
