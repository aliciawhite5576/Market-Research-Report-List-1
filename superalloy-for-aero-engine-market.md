<p><p>Superalloys for the aero engine market mainly consist of three types: Fe-based, Ni-based, and Co-based alloys. Fe-based alloys are characterized by high strength and resistance to corrosion and oxidation. They are commonly used in low-pressure turbine blades and casings. Ni-based alloys offer excellent high-temperature strength, creep resistance, and corrosion resistance, making them suitable for high-pressure turbine components. Co-based alloys possess superior wear resistance and thermal stability, often utilized in combustors and exhaust systems. Each type of superalloy is specifically designed to withstand the demanding conditions of aero engines.</p></p>
