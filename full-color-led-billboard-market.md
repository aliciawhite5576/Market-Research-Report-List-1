<p><p>The Full Color LED Billboard Market is classified into three types: P10, P10-P20, and P20. P10 refers to billboards with a pixel pitch of 10mm, indicating the distance between each LED light on the board. P10-P20 includes billboards with a pixel pitch ranging from 10mm to 20mm, whereas P20 billboards have a pixel pitch of 20mm. These classifications are important as they determine the resolution and image quality of the LED billboards, with smaller pixel pitches providing higher resolution and sharper visuals.</p></p>
