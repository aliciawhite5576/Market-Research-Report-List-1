<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Brake Caliper market comprises two main types: Single Piston Caliper and Multi-Piston Caliper. The Single Piston Caliper is a simpler design featuring only one piston that applies pressure to the brake pad. This type is commonly used in smaller electric vehicles and provides adequate braking performance. On the other hand, the Multi-Piston Caliper incorporates multiple pistons that evenly distribute the braking force across the brake pad, enhancing braking power and performance. This type is typically found in larger and high-performance electric vehicles, delivering superior stopping capabilities.</p></p>
