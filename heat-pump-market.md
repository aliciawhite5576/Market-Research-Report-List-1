<p><strong>The Heat Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air-to-Air</li><li>Air-to-Water</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heat pump market consists of two main types: air-to-air and air-to-water. In air-to-air heat pumps, the device extracts heat from the outside air and transfers it indoors for heating purposes. It also has the ability to reverse the process for cooling in warmer months. On the other hand, air-to-water heat pumps extract heat from the outside air and transfer it to water, which can be used for space heating or domestic hot water. These two market types provide efficient and versatile heating solutions for residential and commercial applications.</p></p>
