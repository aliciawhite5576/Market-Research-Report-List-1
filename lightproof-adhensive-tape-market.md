<p><p>Lightproof adhesive tape is a type of tape that is specifically designed to prevent the transmission of light. There are two main types of lightproof adhesive tape available in the market, namely single-sided shading and double-sided shading. </p><p>Single-sided shading tape is used to block light from one side only. It is commonly used in applications where light needs to be blocked out from one direction, such as in photography studios or darkrooms.</p><p>Double-sided shading tape, also known as two-sided shading tape, is designed to block light from both sides. This type of tape is commonly used in light-sensitive applications where complete darkness is required, such as in the manufacturing of electronic devices or during film processing.</p><p>Both types of lightproof adhesive tape provide an effective solution for blocking out light and ensuring a light-free environment for various purposes.</p></p>
