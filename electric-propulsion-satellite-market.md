<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Electric Propulsion Satellite market comprises different types of propulsion technologies. Gridded Ion Engine (GIE) uses a grid system to accelerate ions for thrust. Hall Effect Thruster (HET) generates propulsion by ionizing and accelerating a neutral gas. High Efficiency Multistage Plasma Thruster (HEMPT) utilizes a multistage process to achieve higher efficiency. Pulsed Plasma Thruster (PPT) produces short plasma pulses for thrust. These technologies enable satellites to navigate and adjust their orbits in space using electric power, offering advantages such as fuel efficiency and longer satellite lifespan.</p></p>
