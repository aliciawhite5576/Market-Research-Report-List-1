<p><p>The marine deck coatings market offers various types of coatings, including water-borne, solvent-borne, and other market types. Water-borne coatings are environmentally friendly and contain water as the primary solvent. They are easy to apply and have low levels of volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Solvent-borne coatings, on the other hand, use organic solvents as the primary solvent. They are known for their durability and resistance to harsh marine environments. The "other" market types may include powder coatings or specialty coatings designed for specific applications or purposes within the marine deck coatings industry.</p></p>
