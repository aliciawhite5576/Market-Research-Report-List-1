<p><p>Membrane position sensors are devices that are used to measure and monitor the position of objects or surfaces. These sensors consist of a flexible membrane that is attached to a rigid support structure. When the membrane is deformed due to a change in position, it causes a change in electrical resistance or capacitance, which can be measured and used to determine the position.</p><p>The membrane position sensors market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
