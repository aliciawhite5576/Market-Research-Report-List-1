<p><p>The racing clutches market offers three main types: Carbon/Carbon, Metallic, and Cerametallic clutches. Carbon/Carbon clutches are known for their exceptional strength and durability, making them ideal for high-performance racing vehicles. Metallic clutches are constructed using metallic friction materials and provide good heat dissipation and stability. They are commonly used in drag racing. Cerametallic clutches combine ceramic and metallic components, providing a balance between durability and heat resistance. These clutches are frequently used in motorsports that require strong clamping force and heat capacity.</p></p>
