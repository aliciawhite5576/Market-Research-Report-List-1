<p><p>The Vascular Surgery Digital Stethoscope market includes two types of stethoscopes: wireless digital stethoscopes and wired digital stethoscopes. Wireless digital stethoscopes are equipped with Bluetooth or other wireless technologies that allow them to transmit the heart sounds to a nearby device, such as a smartphone or computer, providing convenience and freedom of movement for healthcare professionals.
