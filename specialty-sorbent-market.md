<p><p>The specialty sorbent market includes three main types: carbon black, chitosan, and engineered nanomaterials. Carbon black sorbents are highly effective in adsorbing pollutants due to their large surface area and high carbon content. Chitosan sorbents, derived from shellfish, possess excellent adsorption properties and are biodegradable, making them suitable for environmental applications. Engineered nanomaterial sorbents, such as metal oxides and carbon nanotubes, offer superior adsorption capabilities due to their nanoscale size and unique properties. These specialty sorbents play a crucial role in various industries, including environmental remediation, water treatment, and air purification.</p></p>
