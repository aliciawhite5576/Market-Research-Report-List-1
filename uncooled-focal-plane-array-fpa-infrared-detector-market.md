<p><p>Uncooled Focal Plane Array (FPA) Infrared Detector is a type of infrared sensor that consists of an array of detectors integrated into a focal plane array. This makes them more compact, cost-effective, and suitable for various applications, including thermal imaging, surveillance, automotive, and medical.</p><p>The future outlook of the uncooled FPA infrared detector market is promising. The increasing demand for thermal imaging technology in applications such as firefighting, industrial monitoring, and defense is driving the market growth.
