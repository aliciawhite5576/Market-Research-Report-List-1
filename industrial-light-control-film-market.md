<p><ul><li>PET Substrate</li><li>Non-PET Substrate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial light control film market can be categorized into two types based on the substrate material used: PET substrate and non-PET substrate. PET substrate refers to light control films that are manufactured using polyethylene terephthalate. These films offer excellent durability, transparency, and resistance to heat and chemicals. On the other hand, non-PET substrate light control films are made using materials other than polyethylene terephthalate, such as polycarbonate or acrylic. These films may have different properties and characteristics compared to PET substrate films, which can influence their applications and performance in various industrial settings.</p></p>
