<p><p>The commercial vehicle radiator market comprises two main types: aluminum radiator and copper radiator. Aluminum radiators are lighter and more fuel-efficient, offering improved cooling performance. They are commonly used in modern vehicles due to their corrosion resistance and recyclability. Copper radiators, on the other hand, have been widely used over the years and are known for their high conductivity and heat transfer capabilities. While copper radiators may be heavier and less efficient compared to aluminum radiators, they continue to find application in specific industries that prioritize heat dissipation and durability over weight.</p></p>
