<p><p>The bike-sharing service market can be categorized into two main types: dockless and station-based. In a dockless market, bicycles are not required to be parked at specific stations or docking stations. Instead, users can pick up and drop off the bikes almost anywhere within a designated area, using a mobile app to unlock and pay for their rides. Conversely, in a station-based market, bicycles are picked up and returned to specific docking stations. Users access the bikes through a membership card or app, ensuring that bikes are properly parked and available for the next user.</p></p>
