<p><p>EMI film market refers to the market for films that possess electromagnetic interference (EMI) shielding properties. There are two types of EMI film markets: metal-mesh film market and coating film market. Metal-mesh films consist of a thin metal layer that is patterned with a fine grid-like mesh, offering high transparency and superior EMI shielding performance. Coating films, on the other hand, are typically made of different materials, such as conductive polymers or organic coatings, which are applied as a thin layer onto different substrates to provide EMI shielding functionality. Both types of films are widely used in various industries to prevent electromagnetic interference and ensure reliable electronic device performance.</p></p>
