<p><p>The inorganic electrochromic material market is segmented into various types, including WO3, MoO3, TiO3, and others. These materials are used in electrochromic devices and possess properties such as color change when induced with an electric current. Tungsten trioxide (WO3) is widely used due to its high electrochromic efficiency. Molybdenum trioxide (MoO3) offers faster response time and improved stability. Titanium trioxide (TiO3) provides advantages like high coloration efficiency and long cycling stability. Other materials in this market include nickel oxide, tungsten bronze, and cobalt oxide, among others.</p></p>
