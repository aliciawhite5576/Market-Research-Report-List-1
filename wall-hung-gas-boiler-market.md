<p><p>The wall hung gas boiler market consists of two main types: condensing and non-condensing boilers. </p><p>Condensing boilers are more energy-efficient as they capture and recycle waste heat produced during combustion, resulting in lower fuel consumption and reduced greenhouse gas emissions. They are especially suitable for modern heating systems with low return water temperatures.</p><p>Non-condensing boilers, on the other hand, do not capture waste heat and operate at higher temperatures. While they are typically less expensive, they are not as efficient as condensing boilers and can result in higher energy costs over time.</p></p>
