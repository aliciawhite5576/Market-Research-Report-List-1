<p><p>Flexible Manufacturing System (FMS) Solutions refer to automated manufacturing systems that allow for the efficient production of a variety of goods with minimal human intervention. Sequential FMS involves a linear manufacturing process with predetermined sequences, while Random FMS allows for the production of items in a flexible and non-linear manner. Dedicated FMS is designed for mass production of a specific product, Engineered FMS is customized for specific manufacturing needs, and Modular FMS comprises pre-engineered modules that can be combined for various production requirements. These different FMS types cater to the diverse needs of manufacturers.</p></p>
