<p><p>The flexible thermal insulation market includes various types such as NBR (nitrile butadiene rubber) based, EPDM (ethylene propylene diene monomer) based, chloroprene based, and others. NBR-based insulation is known for its excellent resistance to oil and chemicals, making it suitable for applications in industries like oil and gas. EPDM-based insulation offers high resistance to weathering and UV radiation, making it ideal for outdoor installations. Chloroprene-based insulation is known for its flame retardant properties, making it suitable for applications in industries such as automotive and construction. Other types of flexible thermal insulation may include materials like elastomeric foams or combination products with additional properties.</p></p>
