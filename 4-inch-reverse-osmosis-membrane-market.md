<p><p>The 4 Inch Reverse Osmosis Membrane market consists of two types of membranes: Composite Membranes and Asymmetric Membranes. Composite Membranes are made up of multiple layers, typically consisting of a thin skin layer and a porous support layer. These membranes offer high rejection rates and are suitable for a wide range of applications. On the other hand, Asymmetric Membranes have a dense active layer that actively separates impurities. These membranes are known for their high permeability and excellent performance in treating wastewater and desalination processes.</p></p>
