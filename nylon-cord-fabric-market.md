<p><p>The Nylon Cord Fabric market is divided into two main types: Nylon 6 and Nylon 66. Nylon 6 refers to a type of nylon that is made from caprolactam. It is known for its elasticity, strength, and resistance to abrasion. Nylon 66, on the other hand, is made from hexamethylenediamine and adipic acid. It is also highly durable and has excellent heat resistance properties. Both Nylon 6 and Nylon 66 are widely used in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, construction, and apparel.</p></p>
