Additionally, the rising prevalence of chronic diseases, such as cardiovascular disorders and cancer, further fuels the demand for medical imaging procedures, thereby boosting the market for X-ray film scanners.</p><p>Furthermore, the transition from traditional film-based X-ray systems to digital radiography is anticipated to propel the market growth.
