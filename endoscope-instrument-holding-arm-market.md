<p><p>The endoscope instrument holding arm market includes two types: articulated and rail-mounted. Articulated arms are designed with multiple joints, allowing for greater flexibility and easy maneuverability in different directions. They are commonly used in surgical procedures as they provide excellent range of motion. Rail-mounted arms, on the other hand, are fixed along a rail system, providing stability and precise positioning during endoscopic procedures. They are often used in larger operating rooms where multiple devices need to be positioned simultaneously. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring accurate and efficient handling of endoscope instruments.</p></p>
