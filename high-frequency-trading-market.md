<p><p>High-frequency trading (HFT) can be categorized into two main market types: on-premise and cloud-based markets. On-premise HFT refers to trading activities that take place within a physical environment, often using dedicated hardware and internet connections. This ensures low latency and allows for faster execution of trades. Alternatively, cloud-based HFT involves using virtual servers and computing power provided by a third-party service provider. The advantage of cloud-based HFT is its flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness since it eliminates the need for expensive on-premise infrastructure.</p></p>
