<p><p>Medical X-Ray Computer Tomography (CT) scanners are advanced imaging devices used in healthcare to obtain detailed cross-sectional images of the body. The market for CT scanners can be classified into three types: Singledetector CT, Doubledetector CT, and Multidetector CT. Singledetector CT scanners use a single detector to capture X-ray images, offering basic functionality. Doubledetector CT scanners, as the name suggests, utilize two detectors for improved speed and image quality. Multidetector CT scanners employ several detectors simultaneously, resulting in higher resolution, faster scanning times, and enhanced diagnostic accuracy.</p></p>
