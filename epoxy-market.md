<p><p>The epoxy market can be categorized into three main types: liquid, solid, and solution. Liquid epoxy is a versatile form that can be easily applied to various surfaces and is commonly used in coatings, adhesives, and composites. Solid epoxy provides a durable and rigid material that is typically utilized in electrical insulation and structural applications. Epoxy solutions are formed by dissolving epoxy resin in a solvent and are often used in formulations where controlled viscosity is required. These different types cater to diverse industrial needs and applications.</p></p>
