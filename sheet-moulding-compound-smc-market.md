<p><p>Sheet Moulding Compound (SMC) is a type of fiber-reinforced plastic that is widely used in the automotive, construction, electrical, and other industries. It is composed of a mixture of chopped glass fibers, polyester resin, fillers, and additives, which are compounded into a sheet form and then molded using heat and pressure.</p><p>The global SMC market has been experiencing significant growth due to the increasing demand for lightweight and fuel-efficient vehicles. SMC offers several advantages such as high strength-to-weight ratio, excellent corrosion resistance, and ease of processing, making it a preferred choice in the automotive industry.
