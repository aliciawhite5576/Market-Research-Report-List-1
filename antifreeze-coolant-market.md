<p><p>Antifreeze coolant, also known as engine coolant or radiator fluid, is a liquid substance used in vehicles to regulate engine temperature and prevent overheating or freezing in extreme conditions. <p><p>The antifreeze coolant market is categorized into various types based on the chemical composition of the coolant. Methanol, the most common type, offers excellent freeze protection but is toxic. Ethylene glycol is widely used due to its high efficiency and low cost, but it is also toxic. Propylene glycol is less toxic and commonly used in food-grade applications. Glycerol is a non-toxic alternative with lower freeze protection. Other types include blends and additives with various properties like corrosion inhibitors and lubricants.</p></p>
