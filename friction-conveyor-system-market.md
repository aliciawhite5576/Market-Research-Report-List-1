<p><p>A friction conveyor system, also known as a non-powered conveyor system, is a type of material handling equipment that uses friction between the conveyor belt and the object being transported to move items from one location to another. This type of system is commonly used in industries such as manufacturing, distribution, and logistics to facilitate the movement of lightweight or medium-weight goods.</p><p>The future outlook for the friction conveyor system market is expected to be positive. The increasing demand for automation and efficient material handling solutions in industries is driving the adoption of friction conveyor systems. These systems offer several advantages, including low maintenance, easy installation, and cost-effectiveness, which are further contributing to their market growth.</p><p>Additionally, the growth of e-commerce and the rise in online shopping have increased the demand for efficient and fast material handling solutions.
