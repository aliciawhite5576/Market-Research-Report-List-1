<p><p>Bonded Nd-Fe-B magnets are categorized into two markets: Injected Magnets and Pressed Magnets. Injected Magnets are manufactured by injecting a molten mixture of Nd-Fe-B powder and thermoplastic resin into a mold, resulting in complex shapes with high levels of accuracy and dimensional precision. On the other hand, Pressed Magnets are created by compressing a mixture of Nd-Fe-B powder and binder under high pressure, followed by sintering to obtain the desired shape. Although less complex, Pressed Magnets are still highly efficient and widely used in various industries.</p></p>
