Two Phase chargers employ two power sources and offer improved power output for medium-power requirements. Three Phase chargers use three power sources, enabling high-power outputs for heavy-duty applications. These different market types cater to diverse power needs, providing efficient and effective charging solutions for various devices and systems.</p></p>
