<p><p>X-Ray Radiation Detectors are used to measure and detect radiation levels in various applications. The market for these detectors can be classified into four types. </p><p>Flat Panel Detectors are advanced and offer high-resolution imaging capabilities using a thin semiconductor layer. Computed Radiography (CR) Detectors use photostimulable phosphor plates to capture X-ray images. Charge Coupled Device (CCD) Detectors convert X-ray photons into electrical signals for image formation. Other detectors include scintillation detectors, gas detectors, and solid-state detectors, each utilizing different materials and technologies to detect X-ray radiation. These detectors cater to diverse industries, including healthcare, manufacturing, and security.</p></p>
