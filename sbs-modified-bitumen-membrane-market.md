<p><p>SBS Modified Bitumen Membrane is a type of roofing membrane that is commonly used in the construction industry. There are three main types of SBS Modified Bitumen Membrane: Base/Interply Membrane, Base/Ply Membrane, and Cap Sheets. </p><p>- Base/Interply Membrane is a high-strength membrane that is installed between layers of insulation to provide additional reinforcement and durability to the roof system.</p><p>- Base/Ply Membrane is a heavy-duty membrane that is used as the base layer in multi-ply roof systems. It provides excellent waterproofing and protection against weathering.</p><p>- Cap Sheets are the top layer of the SBS Modified Bitumen Membrane system. They are available in various colors and provide additional protection against UV rays and weathering.</p></p>
