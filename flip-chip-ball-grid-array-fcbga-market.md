Bare Die FCBGA refers to the packaging of bare chips directly onto an organic substrate. SiP FCBGA, or System in Package FCBGA, involves the integration of multiple chips, passive components, and other electronic elements within a single package. Lidded FCBGA includes a protective cover over the chips to ensure their safety during transportation and handling. Each market type offers specific benefits and is suitable for different applications.</p></p>
