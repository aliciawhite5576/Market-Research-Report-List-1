<p><p>The Single Door Magnetic Lock Market consists of two types of locks: surface mounted and embedded installation. Surface mounted magnetic locks are installed on the surface of the door frame, providing a visible and easily accessible locking mechanism. On the other hand, embedded installation magnetic locks are concealed within the door frame, creating a more aesthetically pleasing appearance. Both types offer secure locking solutions, but surface mounted locks are preferred for their simplicity and cost-effectiveness, while embedded installation locks are chosen for their enhanced appearance and resistance to tampering.</p></p>
