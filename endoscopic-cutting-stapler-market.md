<p><p>The Endoscopic Cutting Stapler Market consists of three types: Linear, Standard, and Circular. Linear staplers are primarily used for surgeries involving straight or curved incisions where tissues need to be stapled together. Standard staplers are suitable for end-to-end anastomosis or tissue transection during surgeries. Circular staplers are utilized in procedures such as colorectal or gastric surgeries, where stapling of circular structures is required. Each type caters to different surgical needs, ensuring precise and efficient tissue cutting and stapling during endoscopic procedures.</p></p>
