<p><p>The Microwave Landing System (MLS) market is categorized into Type I and Type II markets. Type I MLS is designed for non-precision approaches and is mainly used at smaller airports. It provides horizontal and vertical guidance for aircraft during landing, ensuring safe touchdown without relying on ground-based navigational aids. On the other hand, Type II MLS is designed for precision approaches and is used at larger airports. It provides highly accurate and reliable guidance for precision landings, even in adverse weather conditions, improving overall flight safety.</p></p>
