<p><ul><li>Fixed Wing</li><li>Rotary Wing</li><li>Hybrid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The military UAVs market is categorized into three types: Fixed Wing, Rotary Wing, and Hybrid. Fixed wing UAVs are designed like airplanes and use forward propulsion to fly. They have longer endurance and larger payload capacity. In contrast, rotary wing UAVs, resembling helicopters, are more maneuverable and can hover and fly vertically. Lastly, hybrid UAVs combine the features of fixed and rotary wing UAVs, allowing for both vertical takeoff and landing as well as efficient forward flight. Each type caters to specific military operations and requirements.</p></p>
