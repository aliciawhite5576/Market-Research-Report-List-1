<p><p>The milk refrigeration tank market includes two types: closed type tanks and open type tanks. Closed type tanks are designed with an enclosed structure and provide proper insulation to maintain a cool temperature for milk storage. This type of tank is commonly used to store and transport milk in bulk, ensuring its quality and freshness. On the other hand, open type tanks do not have a closed structure and are mainly used for small-scale milk storage. They are more accessible for manual handling and suitable for use in small dairy farms or households.</p></p>
