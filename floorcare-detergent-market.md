<p><p>Floorcare detergent market can be classified into three main types: decontamination, protect, and others. </p><p>Decontamination floorcare detergents are specifically formulated to deeply clean and remove stubborn dirt, stains, and contaminants from the floor surface. They effectively eliminate bacteria, viruses, and other germs, ensuring a hygienic environment.</p><p>Protect floorcare detergents, on the other hand, are designed to create a protective layer on the floor, shielding it from scratches, scuffs, and other damages. These detergents enhance the floor's durability and extend its lifespan.</p><p>Lastly, the "others" category encompasses a range of specialized floorcare detergents, such as those designed for specific floor types (wood, tile, laminate), odor elimination, or environmentally friendly options.</p></p>
