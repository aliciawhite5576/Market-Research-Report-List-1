<p><p>Marine window wipes are essential for maintaining clear visibility on boats and ships. There are several types of wipers available, including straight line wipers, pantograph wipers, and pendulum wipers. Straight line wipers move in a linear motion, while pantograph wipers use a scissor-like mechanism for wider coverage. Pendulum wipers swing back and forth to clear the window. Each type serves a specific purpose based on the vessel's window design and size. These wipers ensure safe navigation by removing water, dirt, and debris from the windows, guaranteeing better visibility for the crew.</p></p>
