<p><p>The laser patterning equipment market is divided into two main types: Batch Type and Continuous Type. Batch Type equipment is designed to process a limited number of substrates simultaneously in a single operation. It is often used for small-scale production or research and development purposes. On the other hand, Continuous Type equipment is capable of handling a continuous flow of substrates, enabling high-volume production. It is commonly utilized in industrial manufacturing processes where large quantities of products need to be patterned quickly and efficiently.</p></p>
