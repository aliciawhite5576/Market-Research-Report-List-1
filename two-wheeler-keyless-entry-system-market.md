<p><p>The Two-Wheeler Keyless Entry System market consists of two main types: Passive Keyless Entry System (PKES) and Remote Keyless Entry System (RKES). Passive Keyless Entry System enables the user to unlock or start their vehicle without physically interacting with the key fob, using proximity sensors instead. On the other hand, Remote Keyless Entry System requires pressing a button on the key fob to lock/unlock the vehicle from a short distance. Both systems provide convenience and security to users by eliminating the need for a physical key.</p></p>
