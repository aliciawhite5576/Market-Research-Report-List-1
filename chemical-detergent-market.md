<p><p>The chemical detergent market is broadly categorized into three types: acid type, alkaline type, and neutral type. Acid-based detergents are mostly used in industries for removing scale deposits and rust. Alkaline detergents are commonly used for heavy-duty cleaning, such as in commercial kitchens or industrial settings. Neutral detergents, also known as pH-neutral detergents, are suitable for general everyday cleaning tasks in households as they are gentle on most surfaces and fabrics. Each type has its specific application based on its chemical composition and cleaning properties.</p></p>
