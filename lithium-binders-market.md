<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Lithium binders play a crucial role in the production of lithium-ion batteries. Anode binders and cathode binders are the two main types of lithium binders in the market. Anode binders are used to hold the anode material together in the battery, ensuring stability and preventing electrode degradation. On the other hand, cathode binders are employed to enhance the adhesion between the active cathode material and the current collector, improving overall battery performance. These binders are essential for the efficient functioning and durability of lithium-ion batteries.</p></p>
