<p><p>The plastic decking market is categorized into various types, including HDPE, LDPE, PP, PVC, and others (PS and PET). HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) is known for its superior strength and durability, making it a popular choice for decking applications. LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) offers excellent flexibility and impact resistance. PP (Polypropylene) offers high resistance to chemical and UV damage. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) decking is known for its low maintenance and durability. Other types of plastic decking like PS (Polystyrene) and PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) also have unique properties for specific decking needs.</p></p>
