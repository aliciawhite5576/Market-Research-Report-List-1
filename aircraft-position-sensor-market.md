<p><p>The Aircraft Position Sensor market includes two main types: Linear and Angular. Linear sensors measure the linear displacement of an aircraft component and determine its position relative to a reference point. These sensors are commonly used in applications such as wing flap position detection. On the other hand, angular sensors measure the angular displacement of the aircraft's moving parts, such as the rudder or ailerons. These sensors enable the precise monitoring and control of the aircraft's attitude and movement, contributing to flight stability and safety. Both types of sensors play crucial roles in ensuring accurate positioning and control of various aircraft components.</p></p>
