<p><p>The electronic fine ceramic market is categorized into two types: electronic structural ceramics and electronic functional ceramics. Electronic structural ceramics are used primarily for mechanical support and protection in electronic devices. They provide insulation and stability, ensuring the durability and reliability of electronic components. On the other hand, electronic functional ceramics possess special electrical, magnetic, or optical properties that enable them to perform specific functions in electronic devices, such as sensing, actuating, or signal processing. These materials play a crucial role in enhancing the performance and efficiency of electronic systems.</p></p>
