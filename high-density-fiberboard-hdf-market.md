The increasing demand for sustainable and durable building materials, coupled with the growing construction industry, is driving the market growth. HDF is widely used in various construction applications such as flooring, doors, roofing, and wall panels due to its high density and strength.</p><p>In addition, the growing furniture industry is also fueling the demand for HDF as it is an ideal material for manufacturing furniture components like cabinets, shelves, and tabletops. The rising urbanization and changing lifestyle patterns are leading to increased demand for modern and stylish furniture, which in turn is boosting the HDF market.</p><p>Moreover, technological advancements in manufacturing processes have led to the development of improved HDF products with enhanced properties such as moisture resistance, fire resistance, and sound insulation.
