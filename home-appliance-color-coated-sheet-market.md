<p><strong>The Home Appliance Color-Coated Sheet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PCM</li><li>VCM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Home appliance color-coated sheet market includes two main types: PCM and VCM. PCM (Pre-coated Metal) sheets are made by laminating organic paint coating onto metal sheets. They provide excellent resistance against corrosion, scratch, and fading, making them ideal for appliances like refrigerators and washing machines. On the other hand, VCM (Vinyl Coated Metal) sheets are created by laminating a thin PVC film onto metal sheets. VCM sheets offer a wide range of colors and patterns, providing a decorative and durable solution for various home appliances.</p></p>
