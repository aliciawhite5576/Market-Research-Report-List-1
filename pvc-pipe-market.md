<p><p>The PVC pipe market includes various types of pipes that are commonly used in plumbing and construction. Chlorinated PVC pipes are made by chemically treating PVC with chlorine, making them more resistant to heat and chemicals. Unplasticized PVC pipes, also known as rigid PVC pipes, are made without adding plasticizers, making them strong and durable. Plasticized PVC pipes, on the other hand, are made by adding plasticizers to PVC, which makes them more flexible and suitable for applications such as irrigation and drainage. These different types cater to various needs and preferences within the PVC pipe market.</p></p>
