Freeze drying is used to convert liquid nanoencapsulates into powder form, preserving their properties. Nanoprecipitation is a technique used to precipitate nano-sized particles from a solution, resulting in improved stability and controlled release of additives. These applications offer benefits such as improved taste, nutrient delivery, controlled release, and enhanced food product shelf life.</p></p>
