<p><p>NTC (Negative Temperature Coefficient) thermistor temperature sensors are electronic devices used to measure temperature changes by calculating the resistance of a material. <p><strong>The NTC Thermistor Temperature Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>NTC thermistor temperature sensors are available in various market types. Radial type sensors have two leads emerging from opposite sides of a ceramic disc, making them suitable for surface mounting devices. Diode type sensors have three leads, enabling their use in digital temperature detection. Wire bonding type sensors have two leads attached to the same side of a ceramic frame, suitable for automated production processes. Other market types include glass encapsulated sensors, chip type sensors, and ring lug type sensors, each with unique advantages based on their design and application requirements.</p></p>
