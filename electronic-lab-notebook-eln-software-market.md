Additionally, there is a rising demand for ELN software that is compatible with mobile devices, allowing researchers to access and update their data on-the-go.</p><p>Moreover, the market is witnessing the emergence of cloud-based ELN solutions, which offer greater flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness compared to on-premises solutions.
