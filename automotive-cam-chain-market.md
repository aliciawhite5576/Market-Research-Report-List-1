<p><strong>The Automotive Cam Chain Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Roller Chain</li><li>Silent Chain</li></ul></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The automotive cam chain market consists of two main types: roller chain and silent chain. Roller chains are widely used in conventional engines, utilizing cylindrical rollers that engage with sprockets to drive camshafts. They provide smooth operation and high durability, making them suitable for high-performance applications. On the other hand, silent chains employ a toothed link design that reduces noise and vibration. They are commonly used in engines where noise reduction is paramount, such as hybrid or electric vehicles. Both types contribute to the efficient functioning of engines by synchronizing camshaft movement.</p></p>
