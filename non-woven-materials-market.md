<p><p>Non-woven materials are a type of fabric that is manufactured by bonding fibers together rather than weaving them. The healthcare sector is one of the major driving factors for the market, as non-woven materials are widely used in medical products like surgical gowns, masks, and wipes. The increasing awareness about hygiene and healthcare in developing countries is also expected to contribute to market growth.</p><p>Another key factor driving the market is the growing demand for durable and cost-effective materials in the automotive industry. With the increasing focus on sustainability, manufacturers are striving to develop non-woven materials that are environmentally friendly and can be easily decomposed after use.
