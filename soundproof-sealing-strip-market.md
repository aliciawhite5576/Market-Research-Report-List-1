<p><p>The soundproof sealing strip market includes three main types: PVC strips, EPDM strips, and EPDM/PP strips. PVC strips are made from polyvinyl chloride and offer good resistance to wear and tear, making them durable and cost-effective. EPDM strips are made from ethylene propylene diene monomer rubber and are known for their excellent flexibility and weather resistance. EPDM/PP strips are a combination of EPDM rubber and polypropylene, offering enhanced durability and high temperature resistance. These different types cater to various customer needs and preferences in the soundproof sealing strip market.</p></p>
