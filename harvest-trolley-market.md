<p><p>The market for Harvest Trolleys consists of three main types: Self-propelled Harvest Trolley, Mounted Harvest Trolley, and Trailed Harvest Trolley. Self-propelled Harvest Trolleys are equipped with their own power source and are capable of moving independently. Mounted Harvest Trolleys are attached to another vehicle, such as a tractor, and are pulled along. Trailed Harvest Trolleys are directly towed behind a vehicle. These different market types offer farmers options based on their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
