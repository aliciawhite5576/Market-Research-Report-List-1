<p><p>The LED encapsulants market comprises three main types: Epoxides, Polyimides, and Silicones (including RTV, gel, etc.). Epoxides are known for their excellent mechanical properties, heat resistance, and reliability. Polyimides offer outstanding thermal stability and low moisture absorption. Silicones are preferred for their flexibility, easy application, and high resistance to UV radiation. Within silicones, RTV (room-temperature vulcanizing) variants provide flexibility and good adhesion, while gel-type silicones offer enhanced protection against vibrations and shocks. These encapsulants are widely used in the LED industry for protection and enhanced performance of lighting components.</p></p>
