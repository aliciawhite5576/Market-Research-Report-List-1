<p><p>The natural flake graphite market can be classified into three types: medium carbon flake graphite, high carbon flake graphite, and high purity flake graphite. High carbon flake graphite has a carbon content above 90% and is commonly used in foundry coatings and refractories. High purity flake graphite has the highest carbon content of over 99%, making it ideal for advanced applications like nuclear reactors and aerospace components due to its exceptional thermal and electrical conductivity properties.</p></p>
