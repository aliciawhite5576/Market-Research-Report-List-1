<p><p>The Industrial Silicone Fluid market comprises two main types: Straight Silicone Fluid and Modified Silicone Fluid. Straight Silicone Fluid refers to a pure form of silicone fluid, which possesses excellent lubricating properties and high thermal stability, making it suitable for a wide range of industrial applications. On the other hand, Modified Silicone Fluid undergoes chemical modifications to enhance its properties, such as improving water repellency, increasing viscosity, or increasing resistance to extreme temperatures. These modifications enable the Modified Silicone Fluid to fulfill specific requirements in various industries, including automotive, electronics, and cosmetics.</p></p>
