As it is a natural stone, travertine is considered an environmentally friendly option compared to artificial flooring materials. Additionally, the durability and longevity of travertine stone tile make it a popular choice for residential and commercial applications.</p><p>In terms of market analysis, the Asia-Pacific region is expected to dominate the travertine stone tile market.
