<p><p>Healthcare and Medical Analytics can be categorized into three main types: Prescriptive, Descriptive, and Predictive. Descriptive analytics involves analyzing past data to understand what has happened in the healthcare system. Predictive analytics utilizes historical data to identify patterns and make informed predictions about future events or outcomes. Prescriptive analytics takes predictive analysis a step further by using algorithms to recommend specific actions or interventions for optimal patient care and resource allocation. Together, these types of analytics help healthcare professionals improve decision-making, optimize resources, and enhance patient outcomes.</p></p>
