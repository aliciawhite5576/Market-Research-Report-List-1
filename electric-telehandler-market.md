<p><p>The electric telehandler market has two main types: all-electric and hybrid. All-electric telehandlers are powered solely by electricity and do not require any fossil fuels. They are environmentally friendly and produce zero emissions during operation. On the other hand, hybrid telehandlers combine both electric and diesel power sources.
