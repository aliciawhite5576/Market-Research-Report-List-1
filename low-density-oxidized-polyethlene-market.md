The increasing demand for LDPE in various end-use industries, such as packaging, agriculture, automotive, and construction, is driving market growth. LDPE is extensively used in the packaging industry for the production of films, sheets, and bags due to its high flexibility and transparency. In the agriculture sector, LDPE is utilized for mulching, greenhouse films, and irrigation pipes. Additionally, the growing construction activities worldwide are further propelling the demand for LDPE in the form of pipes and sheets.</p><p>One of the emerging trends in the LDPE market is the growing usage of LDPE in the healthcare industry. <p><p>The low density oxidized polyethylene market is categorized into two types: prill and powder. Prill refers to small spherical pellets that are usually generated by a manufacturing process, while powder is a fine substance formed by pulverizing the polyethylene material. Both forms have their advantages and applications. Prill is often preferred for its ease of handling and storage, while powder allows for better dispersion and blending in various industries. The choice between prill and powder depends on the specific requirements of the end-users and the intended application of the polyethylene material.</p></p>
