<p><p>Automatic bleeding valves are devices typically used in heating systems to remove trapped air and maintain water flow. There are four types available. Heating type valves are commonly used in heating radiators and automatically release trapped air. Trace type valves are designed for use in heating systems with long pipelines. Rapid type valves efficiently release air and are suitable for heating systems with unstable conditions. Combined type valves integrate the features of heating and rapid valves, enabling effective air removal in various heating systems.</p></p>
