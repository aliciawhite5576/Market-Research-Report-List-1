<p><ul><li>Master Alarm</li><li>Area Alarm</li><li>Local Alarm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Digital Medical Gas Alarm market consists of three types of alarms: Master Alarm, Area Alarm, and Local Alarm. The Master Alarm is the central alarm system that monitors and alerts healthcare providers about gas supply issues throughout the facility. The Area Alarm is installed in specific areas, such as operating rooms or critical care units, to alert personnel about gas-related concerns within that particular area. The Local Alarm is designed for individual gas outlets or equipment, providing immediate alerts for local users in case of any gas supply abnormalities.</p></p>
