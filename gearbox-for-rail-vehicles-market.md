<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The gearbox for rail vehicles market can be classified into two types: longitudinal drive and transversal drive. In the longitudinal drive market, the gearbox is positioned parallel to the rail tracks, allowing the transfer of power to the wheels along the length of the vehicle. On the other hand, in the transversal drive market, the gearbox is positioned perpendicular to the tracks, transferring power to the wheels transversely. These distinctions determine the direction and efficiency of power transmission in rail vehicles.</p></p>
