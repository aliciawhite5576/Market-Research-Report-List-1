<p><p>The electric recreational vehicle (RV) market consists of two main types: towable RVs and motorized RVs. Towable RVs are designed to be pulled behind a vehicle and include travel trailers, fifth-wheel trailers, and pop-up campers. They offer a range of sizes and amenities to accommodate different preferences and budgets. Motorized RVs, on the other hand, are self-contained units with an engine, typically in the form of a motorhome or camper van. They provide all-in-one mobility and living space, allowing for convenience and flexibility while traveling.</p></p>
