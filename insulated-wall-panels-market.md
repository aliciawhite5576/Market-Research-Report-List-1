<p><strong>The Insulated Wall Panels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polyurethane Insulation Panels</li><li>XPS Insulation Panels</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The insulated wall panels market is classified into two main types: Polyurethane Insulation Panels and XPS Insulation Panels. Polyurethane Insulation Panels are made of rigid foam and provide excellent thermal insulation properties. They are widely used in residential and commercial buildings to enhance energy efficiency. On the other hand, XPS Insulation Panels are made of extruded polystyrene foam and offer high compressive strength. They are commonly used in areas that require better resistance to moisture, such as basements and foundations. Both types of panels contribute to reducing heat transfer, improving building performance, and saving energy costs.</p></p>
