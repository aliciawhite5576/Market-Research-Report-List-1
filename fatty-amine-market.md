<p><p>The fatty amine market can be categorized into three types: primary fatty amine, secondary fatty amine, and tertiary fatty amine. Primary fatty amines are organic compounds with one alkyl chain attached to the nitrogen atom, making them highly reactive and versatile for various applications such as surfactants and emulsifiers. Secondary fatty amines have two alkyl chains attached to the nitrogen atom, offering enhanced solubility and improving their use as corrosion inhibitors and textile softeners. Tertiary fatty amines possess three alkyl chains attached to the nitrogen atom, making them excellent emulsifiers, anti-static agents, and disinfectants.</p></p>
