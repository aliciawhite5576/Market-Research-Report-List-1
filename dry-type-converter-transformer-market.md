<p><p>The Dry Type Converter Transformer market can be categorized into two types: Single-phase and Three-phase. Single-phase transformers have a single primary and secondary winding, suitable for applications with lower power requirements, such as residential and light-commercial systems. Three-phase transformers have three primary and secondary windings, designed for higher power demands, typically used in industrial and commercial systems. Both types eliminate the need for liquid coolant, making them environmentally friendly and easy to maintain. They offer efficient energy conversion and are highly reliable in various power distribution applications.</p></p>
