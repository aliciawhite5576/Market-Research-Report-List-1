DC anesthesia machines are powered by direct current and are suitable for use in areas with low electrical power. Closed anesthesia machines feature a closed circuit system that reduces the need for fresh gas and helps in minimizing the environmental impact. These different types cater to specific requirements and preferences of medical professionals and healthcare facilities.</p></p>
