<p><ul><li>Airtight</li><li>Non-airtight</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rich gas compressor market can be classified into two types - airtight and non-airtight. Airtight compressors are designed to work in a sealed environment, preventing any gas leakage and ensuring optimum efficiency. They are commonly used in applications where gas containment is crucial, such as in the oil and gas industry. On the other hand, non-airtight compressors do not require a sealed environment and are suitable for applications where gas leakage is less critical. These compressors find applications in various industries that prioritize cost-effectiveness over gas containment.</p></p>
