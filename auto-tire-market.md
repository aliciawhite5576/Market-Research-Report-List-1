<p><ul><li>OE Tire</li><li>Replacement Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The auto tire market can be classified into two main types: original equipment (OE) tires and replacement tires. OE tires are those that are installed on vehicles when they are manufactured. These tires are specifically designed and chosen by the vehicle manufacturer to ensure optimal performance and meet the vehicle's specifications. On the other hand, replacement tires are those that are purchased and installed by vehicle owners to replace their original tires. Replacement tires can vary in terms of brand, price, performance, and features, depending on the preferences and needs of the vehicle owner.</p></p>
