<p><p>Feed phytase is an enzyme that is used in animal feed to enhance the digestion of phosphorous and other nutrients, thereby improving overall animal health and performance. Phosphorous is an essential mineral for animals, but it is often found in plant-based feed ingredients in an indigestible form. Feed phytase breaks down the phytate, the main source of indigestible phosphorous in feed, into digestible phosphorous and other nutrients.</p><p>The current outlook of the feed phytase market is positive and is expected to show substantial growth in the future. The increasing demand for high-quality animal protein, along with stringent regulations on the use of inorganic phosphorous sources, is driving the adoption of feed phytase. Furthermore, the growing awareness regarding the environmental impact of excessive phosphorous discharge from animal production systems is also contributing to the market growth.</p><p>The use of feed phytase helps in reducing the dependency on inorganic phosphorous additives, which are not only expensive but also have adverse environmental implications.
